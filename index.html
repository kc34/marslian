Webfarming with Lilian

Like Webfishing, but farming.

How To Play

Controls:
  • WASD to walk around
  • Left click to INTERACT with objects and/or USE a held item
  • Right click to COLLECT a collectable object.
  • Shift to run
  • Tab to cycle through inventory.
  • Goal of the game: IDK
Tips:
  • INTERACT with Home Depot to get a hoe.
  • USE the hoe to place a plot.
  • INTERACT with Pierre's to get a seed.
  • INTERACT with a plot while holding a seed to plant it.
  • INTERACT with the Workshop to get a bow.
  • USE the bow to shoot arrows.
